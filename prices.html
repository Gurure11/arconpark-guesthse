About Us

Welcome to Arcon Park Guest House, nestled in the heart of Vereeniging, where comfort and relaxation meet exceptional service. Our guest house offers a wide range of rooms tailored to suit your needs and preferences, ensuring a memorable stay for all our guests. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, we have accommodations perfect for every occasion. Choose from our cozy double rooms with shared or en-suite bathrooms, or opt for one of our spacious family rooms designed to accommodate groups of three or four. Each room is thoughtfully designed to provide a homely atmosphere, with all the modern amenities you need for a restful stay. Our guests can also enjoy the beautiful outdoor garden, perfect for a peaceful stroll, or take a dip in the sparkling swimming pool, which is complimentary for all guests. For those looking to unwind further, our jacuzzi is available for hire, offering a private, soothing experience. At Arcon Park Guest House, we pride ourselves on offering not only a place to stay but an experience that feels like home. Come and enjoy the warmth of our hospitality, and let us make your stay in Vereeniging a truly special one.
