About the guest house

We’ve brought the main content from the separate prices page onto this one long, elegant scrolling homepage so guests can see everything in one place and book faster.

Arcon Park Guest House offers comfortable, affordable accommodation in Vereeniging with room options suited to different guest needs. Whether you are travelling for business, leisure, or a short stopover, the property provides a homely stay with convenient amenities and welcoming spaces to relax.

Guests can enjoy the outdoor garden, swimming pool, TV with DStv, and free WiFi. There is also a jacuzzi available for hire, making the stay even more enjoyable.
